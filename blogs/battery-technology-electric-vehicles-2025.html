How Electric Cars Work: Battery Technology Explained

  1. Energy Storage: Lithium-ion cells store electrical energy in chemical form
  2. Power Delivery: Battery management systems control power flow to electric motors
  3. Charging Process: EV charging stations convert AC power to DC for battery storage
  4. Regenerative Braking: Kinetic energy is converted back to electrical energy during deceleration
  5. Thermal Management: Advanced cooling systems maintain optimal battery temperature
  6. Safety Systems: Multiple protection layers prevent overcharging and thermal runaway
