The New EV Sales Landscape

Charging infrastructure plays a critical role in this evolution. The availability of EV charging stations and home solutions like the EV home charger has become a decisive factor for many shoppers. Easy access to electric car charging stations alleviates range anxiety, one of the main concerns for first-time electric car buyers. This has also led dealerships to partner with charging providers or even install their own stations to enhance the buying experience.

Key Changes in EV Sales Process

  • Digital-First Approach: 78% of EV buyers research online before visiting dealerships
  • Educational Focus: Sales staff must explain charging, battery life, and incentives
  • Test Drive Evolution: Extended test drives to demonstrate charging capabilities
  • Transparent Pricing: Clear breakdown of incentives, tax credits, and total cost
  • Service Integration: Charging station installation and home charger setup
