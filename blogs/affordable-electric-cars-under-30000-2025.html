Understanding Electric Car Battery Life

Whether shopping for new or used electric cars, the future of driving is electric—and it can be affordable too.

Battery Life Expectations for Budget EVs

  • Warranty Coverage: Most affordable EVs come with 8-year/100,000-mile battery warranty
  • Degradation Rate: Expect 2-3% capacity loss per year under normal conditions
  • Replacement Cost: Battery replacement typically costs $5,000-$15,000
  • Second Life: Used batteries often have 70-80% capacity remaining
  • Maintenance: Minimal maintenance compared to gasoline vehicles

How Do Electric Cars Work: Budget-Friendly Technology

Understanding the technology behind affordable electric cars helps buyers make informed decisions:

Key Components in Budget EVs

  1. Battery Pack: Lithium-ion cells providing 150-300 miles of range
  2. Electric Motor: Single or dual motor setup for propulsion
  3. Power Electronics: Control energy flow between battery and motor
  4. Charging System: Onboard charger for AC charging at home
  5. Thermal Management: Battery cooling and heating systems
