Step-by-Step Installation Process

  1. Site Assessment: Evaluate electrical capacity and optimal charger placement
  2. Permit Application: Obtain necessary electrical permits from local authorities
  3. Electrical Upgrades: Install dedicated circuit and upgrade panel if needed
  4. Charger Installation: Mount and wire the EV home charger
  5. Testing and Commissioning: Verify proper operation and safety features
  6. Final Inspection: Local electrical inspection and approval
