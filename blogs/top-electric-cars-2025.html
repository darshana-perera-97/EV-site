How Do Electric Cars Work?

Understanding electric car technology helps buyers appreciate the benefits of EV ownership:

  1. Battery Pack: Lithium-ion cells store electrical energy for propulsion
  2. Electric Motor: Converts electrical energy to mechanical power for wheels
  3. Power Electronics: Control the flow of electricity between battery and motor
  4. Regenerative Braking: Captures energy during deceleration to recharge battery
  5. Thermal Management: Maintains optimal battery temperature for performance and longevity
